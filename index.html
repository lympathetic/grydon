Medium anchors the family

Grydon is a display typeface drawn in three weights — Medium, Bold and Black. The Medium is its steady anchor: even color, open counters and a calm rhythm that reads well from short paragraphs up to mid-size headings.

Bold carries the hierarchy

The 700 weight is the workhorse for emphasis: section titles, labels and the louder end of a type scale. Stems thicken without smearing detail, so terminals and the ear of the g stay crisp even at small sizes.

Black is the showstopper

At 900 the strokes swell to their heaviest, packing maximum presence into posters, covers and oversized display lines. Counters tighten but stay legible, giving headlines real weight while the ampersand shows off the family’s character.
